Questions?

Is it really anonymous?

Yep. You won't know who recorded your valentine and they won't know it went to you.

How do I know who receives my voice note?

You don't. That's the point. When you submit your voice note our system randomly selects another participant to receive it on Valentine's Day.

When will I get my valentine?

On February 14th! We'll send it to the phone number you provide.

I'm worried about getting a creepy voice note from a stranger.

Fair concern. We monitor submissions and will omit anything that may be considered offensive. That said, we've been doing this for a few years now and have never had to deal with this problem. In our experience, this project has been a wholesome display of the kindness that still exists in the world.

Why do you need my phone number?

So that we can send you an anonymous voice note from another participant on Valentine's Day. You won't hear from us after that. We don't keep your number.

Can I submit more than one voice note?

Yes! The more, the merrier! You're still only getting one back though.
